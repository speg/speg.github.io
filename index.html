Our Story

They say there are plenty of fish in the sea, and one night two such fish met at a local watering hole and what was supposed to be a quick drink turned into an entire evening full of laughter.

They discovered they had both spent time traveling around the globe and soon looked forward to exploring new places together.

They also learned of a mutual love for animals - and recently added a new puppy, Lucy to their family with the already much beloved Winston. Each with their own quirks, they were a perfect match. This spring, during a quiet night at home Steve asked Jessi to spend her life with him, to which she replied "Of course". We hope you will join us at sea to say "I do".
